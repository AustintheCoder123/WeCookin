🍔 Classic Beef Burger Recipe 🔪 Ingredients (Makes 4 burgers): For the patty: 1 lb (450g) ground beef (80/20 for juiciness) 1 tsp salt 1/2 tsp black pepper 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional) 1/2 tsp onion powder (optional) For the assembly: 4 burger buns (brioche, sesame, or your choice) 4 slices cheddar cheese (or any cheese you like) Lettuce leaves Tomato slices Onion slices (red or white) Pickles (optional) Ketchup, mustard, mayo (your choice)

🍳 Instructions: Make the patties: In a bowl, mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and optional spices (don’t overmix). Form into 4 equal patties, slightly larger than your buns (they shrink when cooking). Press a small indent in the center of each patty with your thumb (prevents puffing up). Cook the patties: Pan or grill: Heat over medium-high heat. Add a little oil if using a pan. Cook patties 3–4 minutes per side for medium doneness. In the last minute, add cheese on top and cover to melt. Toast the buns: Lightly butter the insides of the buns and toast them on the pan/grill until golden. Assemble the burger: Bottom bun → Sauce → Lettuce → Patty with cheese → Tomato → Onion → Pickles → Sauce → Top bun.
